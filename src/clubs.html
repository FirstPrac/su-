Club Categories

Explore different student clubs available at our university.

Technology Clubs

Technology clubs focus on programming, robotics, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Students collaborate on innovative projects, participate in hackathons, and attend technical workshops.

Sports Clubs

Sports clubs promote teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Students can join football, cricket, basketball, badminton, and many other sports activities.

Cultural Clubs

Cultural clubs celebrate creativity through music, dance, art, and traditional events. These clubs organize cultural festivals and performances throughout the academic year.

Debate Societies

Debate societies help students develop communication, critical thinking, and public speaking skills through debates, discussions, and competitions.
