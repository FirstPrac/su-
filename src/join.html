How to Join a University Club

Follow these simple steps to become a part of our vibrant community:

Registration Steps

  1. Visit the online portal and log in.
  2. Select the club category you are interested in.
  3. Fill out the registration form.
  4. Attend the orientation session.

Meeting Schedules

Most clubs meet every Wednesday at 4:00 PM in the Student Center.
